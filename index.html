What is NeatData?

NeatData is your ultimate solution for cleaning and preparing your data for manipulation and modeling. Whether you're handling missing values, encoding categorical variables, or performing more advanced data processing, NeatData offers a comprehensive suite of tools to streamline these tasks. You have the flexibility to choose which steps to take or let NeatData automate the process for you. Begin your data treatment journey today and let NeatData assist you every step of the way!
